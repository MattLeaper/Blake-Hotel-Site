Welcome to The Blake Hotel

Fantastic local pub at the top of Blake Street (Sheffields second steepest road!). Winner Sheffield North Pub of the Year 2022 and Sheffield CAMRA Pub of the Month February 2019 as well as the top rated pub in Sheffield in 2024 (Sheffield Star)

Our events include!

Weekly Wednesday quizes at 8.30 PM, entry per team £1 with the winning team securing 8 FREE drink tokens (Up to £6 per token).

As well as multiple monthly folk music nights to enjoy!
